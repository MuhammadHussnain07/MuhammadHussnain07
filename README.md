##  Real Problems I've Solved

---

### 💈 Salon had no booking system → Built AutoLead

> **The Problem:** Owner was managing all appointments on WhatsApp. Missing bookings daily. No record of customer history. Staff spending 2+ hours a day on manual coordination.
>
> **What I Built:** Customer opens app → selects service → enters details → **n8n workflow fires instantly** → confirmation email sent automatically → data saved to Google Sheets → synced to Firebase → admin sees all appointments in real-time dashboard → contacts client on WhatsApp in one tap.
>
> **Result:** Zero missed bookings. Zero manual data entry. 2+ hours saved daily. Owner manages everything from their phone.

---

### 🛒 Business had no mobile store → Built ShopFlow

> **The Problem:** Local business was losing sales to competitors who had apps. No way to take mobile orders. Staff manually handling every inquiry via WhatsApp.
>
> **What I Built:** Full dual-sided e-commerce app — customer app with product catalog, cart, checkout & real-time order tracking + separate admin panel for order management and inventory.
>
> **Result:** Business now takes mobile orders 24/7 without any staff involvement.

---

### 🏥 Clinic losing revenue to no-shows → Built MediBook

> **The Problem:** Clinic was losing 30%+ of appointment revenue to no-shows. Staff spending hours on phone calls for reminders. Paper registers impossible to search or track.
>
> **What I Built:** Patients book from app → automated SMS/email reminders sent 24hrs and 1hr before → doctor manages full daily schedule from phone dashboard — replacing 100% of phone calls and paper.
>
> **Result:** Fewer empty slots, less admin chaos, happier patients, and recovered revenue from reduced no-shows.

---

### ⛽ Fleet owner couldn't track fuel costs → Built Fuco &nbsp; [![Play Store](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live%20on%20Play%20Store-34A853?style=flat-square&logo=google-play&logoColor=white)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ainigmagames.Fuco)

> **The Problem:** Fleet owner had no visibility into where fuel budget was going. Drivers self-reporting on paper. Impossible to detect waste or theft.
>
> **What I Built:** Tracks fuel cost per trip with driver accountability, monthly/weekly/daily analytics, live petrol/diesel/CNG prices, Google Maps navigation — everything from one app.
>
> **Result:** Owner now sees exactly where every dollar of fuel budget is going. Full accountability across every driver and vehicle.

---

### 🎨 3D artist had no platform to sell models → Built ShapeShift &nbsp; [![Play Store](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live%20on%20Play%20Store-34A853?style=flat-square&logo=google-play&logoColor=white)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.enigma4d.crafthub3d)
